Why this exists

Built for CA work, not casual chatting.

Usage workflow

Four steps to get a usable draft.

Do not over-write the first instruction. Give the matter, period and expected output. The GPT will ask for missing details.

Open the GPT

Click the hero button on this page. Confirm that the screen title says CA Prompt Engineering.

State the task plainly

Use a starter card or type a direct task such as: Draft client checklist for Tax Year 2026-27.

Answer the quick menu

Reply with short facts or numbered options. Example: 1, 2, 1. Avoid long background unless requested.

Review and refine

Check the verify-list, then ask for shorter, formal, Hindi, WhatsApp or checklist.

Operating checks

1 Use the direct GPT link or pinned GPT entry. This opens the assistant instead of a generic ChatGPT chat.
2 Verify identity before work. Check the title, author line, logo and description on the landing screen.
3 Start clean for each matter. Use a new GPT chat when the client, notice, AY, GST period or engagement changes.

Responsible CA use

What it will and will not do.

The GPT is a drafting and reasoning assistant. It supports practitioner judgement; it does not replace it.

✓

It will help you

Draft client emails, WhatsApp messages, internal notes and checklists; read pasted notices; prepare response frameworks; compute with workings shown; flag deadlines, assumptions and items to verify.

!

It will not replace you

It does not file returns, access live portals, issue a UDIN or certificate, give final settled legal advice, or send advice directly to your client. Final responsibility remains with the professional.
